ABOUT US!!
Hi!! We are Purnima, Chirag ,Aditya and Carol. Purnima and Chirag have completed their under graduation in electronics and communications and Aditya has completed his under graduation in Information Technology. All of us three are from India. The project is for "Feed My Starving Children" which is a Christian non-profit organization committed to help hungry people who cannot afford food by hand-packing meals specifically formulated for malnourished children, and shipping these meals to their distribution partners. Feed My Starving Children meals have reached nearly 70 countries around the world through missionary partnerships at orphanages, schools, clinics, refugee camps and malnourishment centers.Some of the countries that FMSC serves include Afghanistan, Angola, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Namibia, Nepal, Nicaragua, Togo, Trinidad, Uganda, Ukraine and Niger etc.
